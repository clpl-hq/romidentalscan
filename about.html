About Romi Dental Scan

Romi Dental Scan is a premier dental imaging center located in the heart of Kalkaji, Delhi. We are dedicated to providing dentists and patients with the highest quality diagnostic imaging services, including state-of-the-art CBCT scans and OPG X-rays.
