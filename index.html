Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our dental imaging services and procedures

CBCT (Cone Beam Computed Tomography) is an advanced 3D dental imaging technology that provides detailed views of teeth, bones, nerves, and soft tissues. It's essential for accurate diagnosis, implant planning, root canal treatment, and detecting hidden dental issues that regular X-rays might miss.

Most dental scans are quick and painless. A CBCT scan typically takes 20-40 seconds for the actual scan, while an OPG X-ray takes about 15-20 seconds. The entire visit, including preparation and report generation, usually takes 30-45 minutes.

Yes, dental imaging is very safe. We use state-of-the-art equipment with optimized protocols that minimize radiation exposure. A CBCT scan uses significantly less radiation than medical CT scans, and we follow ALARA principles (As Low As Reasonably Achievable) to ensure patient safety.

While we accept walk-ins, we recommend booking an appointment to minimize wait times. You can call us at +91 92891 09107 or WhatsApp us to schedule your visit. We offer flexible timing throughout the week, including Sundays.

We provide same-day reports for most scans. Digital reports are usually ready within 5-6 hours and can be shared via WhatsApp or email. For complicated cases requiring detailed analysis, reports are provided within 24-48 hours.

Our pricing is competitive and transparent. Costs vary based on the type of scan and area covered. Please call us at +91 92891 09107 for current pricing. We accept multiple payment methods including cash, cards, and UPI.
